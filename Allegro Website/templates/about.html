Allegro Services Inc is an affiliate of the company in Uzbekistan, Allegro Auto LLC which was the dealer of Hyundai Distribution Uzbekistan in western region of Uzbekistan between 2020-2024, and the dealer of BYD Distribution Uzbekistan between 2023-2024. The long-term vision of Allegro Services Inc is to gradually grow the business and expand the territories to export high tech vehicles from Canada, USA, Mexico, EU and UAE.

Allegro Services Inc has been authorised as the dealer to sell armouring vehicles of INKAS Canada since 2025.

Allegro Export is proudly OMVIC-registered. We are legally authorized under the Ontario Motor Vehicle Dealers Act as a certified Exporter. Our registration follows strict guidelines to ensure transparency, consumer protection, and full compliance with Canadian vehicle export regulations.
